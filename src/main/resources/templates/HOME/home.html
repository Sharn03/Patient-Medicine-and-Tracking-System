WHY NMC ?

At NMC Healthcare, our ethos is that top-notch health care is the synergy of timeliness, accessibility and life-enhancing medical expertise. Interlacing convenience in medical care is an innovation that takes the focus solely in providing a patient-centric experience.

NMC accredits and certifies a wide range of health care organizations and manages the evolution of HEDIS®, the performance measurement tool used by more than 90 percent of the nation’s health plans. HEDIS is the measurement tool used by the nation’s health plans to evaluate their performance in terms of clinical quality and customer service.

ABOUT US

New Medical Center, A Multi-Speciality Hospital strives hard for its excellence in hospital care, advocacy and teaching by continuously challenging the existing boundaries in community health.

We serve as the best in diagnostic and therapeutic services within the excellent atmosphere of care, concern and compassion.

Our main vision is to be a prolific leader in the health care sector by providing high end services to the community and society. Furthermore, we constantly create a positive healing environment by combining the medical and caring skills of our staff for the purpose of serving the community.

Our main motive is to extend hands to the community through activities and create awareness of basic health and safety. Apart from this, we work together as a team to strive hard to create a friendly and caring environment for our patients, by making them feel welcome and courteous at all times, even under great difficulties.

Here, we will make sure to provide the highest-quality medicine in accordance with the recommendations of the most experienced doctors.
